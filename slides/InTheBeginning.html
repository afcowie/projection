Welcome to Projection

This slide a test case that I used while developing my HTML-based slide tool.

It's primary purpose is to test various edge cases, but to make it other than entirely contrived its visual elements are adapted from a presentation I gave as the invited speaker at Mukthi '07, the annual conference run by the Linux User Group at the MS Ramaiah Institute of Technology at Bangalore, India. Pretty damn cool that the Mathematics, Computer Science, and Engineering faculty there supports their own LUG and encourages students to participate.

This slide is actually a comment; indeed, you wouldn't ordinarily expect this much text on a slide; it's not like anyone in the audience would be able to read it.

Comment slides are not for display when you're delivering the presentation live; rather, they're meant to help people reading your presentation on the web after the fact (in a future version of Projection I'd like to find a way for slides marked with the “comment” class to be excluded. That's a TODO for now).
