About lists

Everyone hates boring slides with nothing but bullet point lists. And yet almost every presentation you see ends up having boring lists of bullet points. Death by PowerPoint.

The problem with lists is that there is no distinction between the individual items; it's just a continuous undifferentiated visual block.

If you do have to drop a list on people, what can you do to make it interesting? The next two slides offer an example. The first is what not to do; the second one shows what Projection's default theme offers to help; bumping the size up is also a good idea.
